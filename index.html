What We Do

Conservation projects take a long time. They need continual funding to operate, however, most people cannot afford to do this, but they want to help. Adonis offers an alternative way to help. By installing Adonis, regular ads will be replaced with ads from our patrons, who pay conservation projects directly to display those ads. This means that by simply browsing the web, you are, in a tangible way, helping stop animal trafficking and protect the environment.

These ads respect your privacy and your time. They are lightweight, unobstrusive, and will never track your browsing behavior. You can choose to see facts about endangered wildlife, fun facts about animals, pictures of cute animals, or all of them. The choice is all yours.

How It Works

Normally, companies pay ad networks to display their ads. Adonis blocks those ads, and shows ads from our patrons instead. When those ads are displayed, those patrons pay our partner conservation organizations directly. This means that by installing Adonis, you are funding conservation projects.

Features

We highly respect your ability to choose so we created Adonis to be as versatile to your needs as possible.

You can decide how often they want to see our ads, all the way from once to week to every time a page loads.

You can choose the complexity of your ads, whether you want to only see text, images, or both.

You can choose the content of the ads, maybe you want just cute animal pictures and fun facts, or perhaps you want awareness facts as well.

You can choose the style of the text from the modern sans-serif look, or more traditional serif.
