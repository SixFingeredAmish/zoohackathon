About Us

What We Do

Adonis replaces web ads with unintrusive ads supporting the Illegal Wildlife Fund Initiative. The initiative sponsors projects that reduce demand for illegal wildlife products.

You only need to install Adonis on Chrome and you are help supporting the initiative! There is no donation necessary!

How It Works

Corporate patrons pay for ads supporting the Illegal Wildlife Fund through Adonis instead of paying ad companies for promotion.
